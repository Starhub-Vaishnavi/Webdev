About Me

I’m Vaishnavi Reddy, a passionate and curious learner currently pursuing my B.S. degree from IIT Madras. My journey so far has been driven by curiosity, creativity, and a desire to make technology more intuitive and meaningful. I take pride in my ability to blend technical precision with artistic expression, whether I’m designing interfaces, solving problems, or collaborating on projects.
